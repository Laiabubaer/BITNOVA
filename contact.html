FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about Safex.

What is a seed phrase and why is it important?

Your seed phrase is 12 or 24 random words that represent the master key to your wallet. Anyone with these words can access your Bitcoin — keep them written down and stored somewhere safe, offline, and private.

I lost my phone. Can I recover my wallet?

Yes — as long as you have your seed phrase. Download Safex on any new device, tap "Restore Wallet," enter your words in the correct order, and your full wallet and balance will be back instantly.

Is Safex really free?

Yes, completely free — no subscriptions, no hidden fees, no in-app purchases. The only fees you pay are standard Bitcoin network (miner) fees when you send transactions, which go to the Bitcoin network, not to us.

Does Safex collect my data?

No. During normal use, Safex collects zero personal data. We have no account system, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

Can I have multiple wallets?

Yes — you can create or import as many Bitcoin wallets as you need, all managed from within the same Safex app. Each wallet has its own separate seed phrase and balance.

How do I send Bitcoin?

Tap "Send," enter the recipient's Bitcoin address (or scan their QR code), enter the amount, review the details carefully, and confirm. The transaction is broadcast to the Bitcoin network immediately.
